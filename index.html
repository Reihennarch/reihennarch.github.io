About Us

Reinnar Inc is a Hoyoverse-owned creative studio dedicated to building immersive worlds and stories that transcend the boundaries of traditional RPGs. Founded on the belief that emotion and imagination should be at the core of every experience, Reinnar brings together dreamers, artists, and engineers to shape universes that feel alive—where every detail has purpose and every moment tells a story.

Our journey began as a small team obsessed with blending fantasy, philosophy, and technology. Now, under the guidance of Hoyoverse, we stand at the frontier of interactive storytelling—pushing the limits of visual artistry, atmospheric music, and emotionally intelligent gameplay.

We create not just games, but worlds—places where light and shadow intertwine, and where players can lose themselves in the beauty of discovery. Every project we craft follows one principle:

“Reality ends where imagination begins.”
